SPACE SOLUTIONS

Advanced space technology, science systems and mission services that power commercial, government and scientific missions from low-Earth orbit to deep space.

Satellite Bus / Main Frame

Modular, mission-ready platforms from CubeSats to microsats—engineered for fast integration and reliable operations. We optimize mass, power, and thermal from day one, so you launch on schedule and scale your constellation without rework.

ADCS
Attitude Determination & Control

Precision pointing and agile slews with robust safe-mode behavior. Our sensor-fusion and control algorithms deliver stable imaging, tight jitter performance, and smooth maneuvering—so your payload always gets the shot.

Power Subsystems
(EPS/PCDU)

High-efficiency MPPT, smart battery management, and fault-tolerant distribution in a compact footprint. Designed for continuous uptime with load shedding, inrush control, and telemetry that keeps you ahead of anomalies.

Communication Subsystems

From resilient UHF command links to high-rate S/X-band downlinks, we deliver clean RF chains and SDR flexibility. Link-budgeted, tested, and ground-segment ready—so your data flows the first time and every time.

Antennas

Deployable and fixed antennas tuned to your mission bands for maximum gain and pattern stability. We handle the full chain—EM simulation, matching, and environmental testing—to keep links locked in orbit.

OBC
(On-Board Computer)

Radiation-tolerant compute with secure boot, watchdogs, and real-time tasking. Fast integrations via clean APIs and flight-proven middleware* that keep the bus responsive and your software updates painless

Structure

Lightweight, stiff, and thermally disciplined frames using space-grade aluminum and composite options. DFM-ready for local manufacturing, with smart harnessing and access panels that make AIT fast and frustration-free.
